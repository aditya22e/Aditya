GDP

GDP is an innovative and dynamic student committee dedicated to

fostering growth, skill development, and leadership across multiple domains.

The committee is divided into five specialized departments,

each working towards creating an impactful and collaborative environment

for students to grow professionally and personally

GDP visions towards becoming a pan-India level platform to

provide students a place to showcase their skills, create connections,

get better in their respective domain and get a place to collaborate

with others, since the idea of GDP wouldn't have been in existence

without collaboration, we also focus on person to get better grips

over Leadership and movement towards overall Development.

we believe in growth through collaboration and innovation.
